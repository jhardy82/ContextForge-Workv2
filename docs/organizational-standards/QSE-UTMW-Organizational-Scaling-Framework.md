## QSE UTMW Methodology Overview

### The 8-Phase Universal Workflow

| Phase | Name | Purpose | Key Deliverables |
|-------|------|---------|------------------|
| 0 | Session Init | Context establishment and correlation setup | Session log, correlation ID |
| 1 | Scoping & Alignment | Define objectives and success criteria | TaskScope, ScopeCheck |
| 2 | Research & SME Study | Knowledge acquisition and expertise building | ResearchPlan, KnowledgeDepth |
| 3 | Plan & Design | Comprehensive execution planning | ExecutionPlan, Rationale |
| 4 | Validation & Confidence | Risk assessment and confidence building | TripleCheck, ConfidenceReport |
| 5 | Integration & Sync | System alignment and coordination | SyncReport, MemoryAlignment |
| 6 | Execution & Artifact Generation | Implementation and deliverable creation | Artifacts, EvidenceBundle |
| 7 | Test Creation & Execution | Quality validation and testing | TestResults, GatePassMatrix |
| 8 | Reflection & AAR | Learning capture and knowledge retention | AAR, LessonsLearned |

### Core Principles
1. **Evidence-First**: All work maintains correlation IDs and evidence trails
2. **User-Centric**: Immediate response to feedback with 5-phase integration
3. **Systematic Execution**: Structured phases with clear deliverables
4. **Continuous Improvement**: Quantitative validation enables optimization
5. **Quality Gates**: Each phase has validation criteria before progression
